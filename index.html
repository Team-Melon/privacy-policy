Privacy Policy for Torch Guy - Google Play Game

Welcome to Torch Guy, a thrilling game created by Team Melon! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our game "Torch Guy" available on the Google Play Store. Your privacy is important to us. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the game.

1. Information We Collect

We want to assure you that Torch Guy does not collect any personal or sensitive information from its users. The only information collected pertains to the online leaderboard functionality:

  • Leaderboard Information: When users choose to participate in the online leaderboard, the game collects a username or alias chosen by the user, along with the user's score.

2. How We Use Your Information

We do not use, share, or store any personal information provided by users in the online leaderboard. The leaderboard functionality is designed solely for competitive and social purposes within the game.

3. Online Leaderboard

Torch Guy features an online leaderboard where users can submit their scores. This leaderboard is:

  • Protected: The online leaderboard is protected by robust security measures to prevent unauthorized access or tampering.
  • Encrypted: All data transmitted between the game and the online leaderboard server is encrypted to ensure confidentiality.
  • Profanity Filter: The leaderboard content is monitored and filtered to prevent the display of any inappropriate or offensive language.
  • User Control: Users have the option to delete their entries from the leaderboard at any time, ensuring their information is removed from public view instantly.

4. Children's Privacy

Torch Guy is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove such information.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Team Melon Mobile reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting the information we collect.
